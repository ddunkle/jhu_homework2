About Me

I grew up in a small town in NW Ohio. After graduating from Bowling Green State University, I moved to Maryland and taught high school math for 10 year. My focus was in Geometry and Calculus. I then joined a small mortgage company where I have been for the past 5 years.

My goal is to learn full stack development so that I can assist my company and modernize our workflow. While my end goal is not to be the person doing the development, I am trying to learn more about the process so that I can have a better working understanding of what the development entails.
